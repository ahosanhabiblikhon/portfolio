About Me

Passionate About Science
& Technology

Where molecular biology meets the digital frontier

I'm Ahosan Habib Likhon, a BSc student in Biotechnology & Genetic Engineering at Islamic University, Bangladesh. My academic journey has given me a deep appreciation for the elegant complexity of life at the molecular level — from DNA replication to gene expression, every mechanism is a marvel of biological engineering.

Beyond the laboratory, I'm driven by the immense potential of computational approaches in biology. Bioinformatics and AI are transforming how we analyze genomes, predict protein structures, and develop targeted therapies. I'm actively building skills in these areas to contribute to this exciting frontier.

Parallel to my scientific pursuits, I'm developing as a front-end developer, creating digital experiences that communicate science beautifully. I believe that clear, compelling interfaces are essential for sharing research with the world.

My creative side expresses itself through photography — capturing the subtle beauty of nature, people, and the world around me with my camera and mobile device.
